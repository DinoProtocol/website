Dino Protocol

Dino is 50% meme and 50% index that tracks the oldest coins & blockchains in existence. Think buying Dogecoin at 10m mcap. Dino Protocol bets on the hypothesis that Dino coins (coins & chains operating for 9+ years) will increase in value over time with little to no downside. Dino Protocol makes it easy to own the oldest existing tokens without operating nodes, managing local wallets & registering on obscure exchanges.

What is Dino Protocol?

Dino Protocol is a basket mechanism for “OG” coins such as NMC, PPC, VTC, NAV, TRC, FTC, NVC & others. Dino Protocol executes trades and rebalances its holdings automatically on behalf of DINO token holders. The protocol functions as a DAO, with DINO holders owning the underlying assets and voting on the asset composition. The protocol is a set of onchain/offchain scripts to automatically rebalance using DEXs/CEXs such as Uniswap, Sushiswap, Bittrex, Poloniex, CoinEx, HitBTC, SouthXchange, FreiExchange, and others.

Why Dino Coins?

In short: Historical value/proof of time, Lindy effect, less risk of being hacked, collector value/liquid NFTs, oldest running blockchains, simple but unique value propositions, better distributed (usually 10k+ addresses), not securities (passing Howey test), no ICO/premine, no VCs/whales, previous top 5/10/20, meme potential (Doge was a Dino coin until discovered), some still actively developed, owned by community/foundation, listing potential, market cap potential (some of the oldest blockchains are currently at 100k market cap). How does it work?

Dino Protocol rebalances periodically based on DINO token buys/sells. When a user purchases DINO, half of the position value is converted into ETH by the smart contract and used to increase the underlying asset holdings. When the user sells DINO, half of the position value is converted from underlying assets to ETH and used to buy back DINO automatically. The buy/sell of underlying assets is determined by the amount of DINO/ETH held by the smart contract. When the contract holds more DINO (reserve DINO value > ETH value), underlying assets are sold and used to increase the ETH held by the contract. When the contract holds more ETH, the surplus ETH is used to purchase underlying assets.

Coin Composition & Performance

Currently, the index consists of 50% DINO (half of the purchased DINO value is priced based on supply/demand for DINO, while the other half is priced based on its underlying asset value), 8% NMC, 7% FTC, 7% VTC, 7% PPC, 7% NAV, 7% TRC, 7% NVC. Tokenomics

70% of the DINO supply is held by the smart contract and serves as collateral & mechanism used to purchase the underlying tokens. 25% of the supply is added to liquidity on Uniswap. 5% is held by the team and used for future payments, such as listings and development. There is a 0% buy tax and a 16% sell tax which automatically increases liquidity. Liquidity was locked and the contract is renounced.
